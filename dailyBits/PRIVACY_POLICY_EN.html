Privacy Policy for DailyBits

Last Updated: May 2024

Welcome to DailyBits. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our "Local-First" approach to data security.

1. Data Collection Philosophy

DailyBits is designed as a secure, private vault for your thoughts. Your journals, images, and audio recordings are stored only on your local device. We do not have access to your personal content.

2. App Permissions

  • Camera: To take photos for your entries.
  • Microphone: For voice-to-text features.
  • Storage: To save media files locally.
